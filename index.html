My Story

I've always been drawn to one question: why do people think and act the way they do ‚Äî and how can we build technology that truly serves them?

In the IDF, I served as a Staff Sergeant, leading teams in high-pressure environments. That's where I learned that clear communication, psychological safety, and honest feedback are the real infrastructure of any successful team ‚Äî and any successful product.

Curiosity about the human mind led me to study Psychology and Computer Science at the Open University of Israel. Between 2021‚Äì2023, I worked in cognitive neuroscience research at the Technion and the Hebrew University, running experiments, analyzing data, and turning noisy human behavior into clean, testable models.

More recently, I've moved closer to the business and product side. I've worked with Adi Ohayon Hospitality Consulting on revenue and pricing analytics for hotels and resorts, building dashboards and forecasts that decision-makers actually use. In parallel, I work as a consultant at Deloitte Israel, implementing data-driven systems and learning how large organizations adopt new tools at scale.

The common thread across all of this: I sit at the intersection of psychology, data, and product. I enjoy taking messy, human problems, structuring them, and then building simple, trustworthy tools on top.

My vision is to grow into a Product Architect who bridges technical innovation and real user needs ‚Äî designing AI systems that are powerful under the hood, but feel natural, humane, and obvious to the people using them.

My Product Process

From vague problem to shipped solution

Human-Centric Discovery

I start by understanding the human behavior behind the problem. Using my psychology background, I conduct user interviews and map out emotional journeys, not just functional requirements.

Data Validation

I validate assumptions with hard data. I use Python (Pandas) and SQL to analyze trends, and tools like Jira to structure the roadmap based on evidence, not guesses.

System Architecture

Designing scalable solutions. I sketch flows in Figma and plan the tech stack‚Äîwhether it's SAP BTP for enterprise or FastAPI/React for agile products.

Rapid Build (MVP)

Shipping to learn. I believe in getting a working prototype into users' hands quickly to test the core value proposition and gather real-world feedback.

Data-Driven Iteration

Refining based on impact. I track metrics (Revenue, Retention) and use A/B testing to continuously improve the product, ensuring it delivers measurable business value.
